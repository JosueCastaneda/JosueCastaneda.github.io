My interests

I hold a Master's Degree in Computer Science from the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica (INAOE) which is a Mexican research institute. I am interested on distributed systems, formal methods and mobile sensor networks. My master thesis was supervised by the doctors: Saúl Pomares Hernandez, Jesus A. Gonzalez and José Roberto Pérez Cruz. My thesis on bio-inspired data foraging relates the abstract notion of indirect communication with the spatial and temporal causality of events in order to identify regions of interest in an unknown and dynamic environment.
